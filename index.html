AI-based Medical Imaging Lab

Pusan National University

I am a Full-time research fellow at AI-based Medical Imaging LAB(AMILAB). I received my Master’s degree in Information Convergence Engineering from Pusan National University, where I was fortunate enough to be advised by Prof. MinWoo Kim.

My research interests lie in the application of artificial intelligence in medical imaging. Specifically, my research focuses on improving the generalization of models in various medical image analysis tasks, including image generation, enhancement, and segmentation.

Apart from my research, I like to play tennis and go running.
