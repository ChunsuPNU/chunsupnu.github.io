AI-based Medical Imaging

Pusan National University

I am a associate researcher at AI-based Medical Imaging LAB(AMILAB). I received my Master’s degree in Information Convergence Engineering from Pusan National University, where I was fortunate enough to be advised by Prof. MinWoo Kim.

My research interests lie in the application of artificial intelligence in medical imaging. Specifically, my research focuses on improving the generalization of models in various medical image analysis tasks, including image enhancement, segmentation, and generation.
